# Provide-Insights-to-Chief-of-Operations-in-Transportation-Domain
A comprehensive analysis of cab service performance across key metrics, leveraging Power BI and SQL. Includes actionable insights, recommendations, and dynamic dashboards tailored for tourism and business-centric cities. Explore trends, optimize strategies, and enhance customer satisfaction with data-driven decision-making.



# 🚖 **Project Overview**


This initiative focused on analyzing operational and customer performance data for **Goodcabs**, a cab service operating across tier-2 cities in India.

The business required quick insights into key metrics such as:

* Trip volume
* Passenger satisfaction
* Repeat passenger rates
* Revenue performance
* City-wise demand trends

When the Chief of Operations requested a rapid analysis, I took the initiative to interpret the data and deliver a clear, insight-driven report.



# 📌 **Problem Statement**



Founded two years ago, **Goodcabs** has built a strong presence in India by focusing on tier-2 cities. The company empowers local drivers to earn sustainably while delivering high-quality transportation services to passengers.

With operations across 10 cities, Goodcabs aims to:

* Achieve ambitious growth targets
* Improve passenger satisfaction
* Optimize operational performance in 2024

# 📈 **Key Insights**



* Jaipur recorded the highest number of trips, reaching nearly **77K**, while Mysore had the lowest at **16K**.

* The overall trip growth rate increased by **17.20%**.

* Weekend demand showed significant growth in tourism-focused cities.

* February emerged as the highest-demand month, whereas June recorded the lowest demand.

* Customers from Jaipur and Surat demonstrated higher loyalty rates compared to other cities.

* The Revenue Per Ride (RPR) reached **25.73%**.
